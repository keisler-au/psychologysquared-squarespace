The Ultimate Logbook

The Ultimate Logbook for 5+1 Provisionals

If you are a 5+1 provisional psychologist in Australia, you need to keep a logbook of all your hours as part of your internship. We have created a time saving, easy to use, Excel formatted document, with a lot of smart features, which includes auto totalling for:

  • Client contact data recording with multiple drop down lists

  • Client related tracking including auto totalling hours

  • PD tracking with space for notes and reflective comments

  • Supervision recording (3 per week available)

  • AHPRA 6 monthly reporting calculations

Easy clickability

Instant progress report numbers

Calculator of supervision ratio

Supervisor summary page

Supervision & PD tracking tabs

Dashboard to monitor progress

Client direct/related suggestions

Look at our amazing features and how easy they make logbook work

Just click to add your clients

Reduce your need for tedious manual entries with user-friendly drop-down boxes that save time and reduce errors. Generate clear and accurate reports for your 6-month progress reports to AHPRA with just a few clicks. No more juggling multiple spreadsheets - The Ultimate Logbook automatically calculates and stores all necessary information in a single weekly spreadsheet.

Just click to add client direct and client related activities

Track cumulative totals for all internship aspects, including client-direct hours, supervision sessions, and professional development activities. Gain valuable insights into your progress with automatically generated statistics and graphs. The Ultimate Logbook even estimates your internship completion date and calculates daily hour requirements to keep you on track.

Automatic progress report generator

Generate calculations for 6-month progress reports to AHPRA. Take control of your internship workload with a built-in task tracker. Manage all your tasks including: case reports, observations and assessments, ensuring nothing slips through the cracks. Prioritise tasks, set deadlines, and track your progress for a well-organised and stress-free experience.

Amazing dashboard to track your progress and estimate the end of your internship

The logbook enables users to track cumulative totals, providing a comprehensive overview of their internship progress. The feature allows for effortless monitoring of tasks, sessions, and responsibilities, facilitating a thorough and organised approach to internship documentation. Cumulative tracking empowers users to make informed decisions by offering insights into their achievements and areas for improvement.
